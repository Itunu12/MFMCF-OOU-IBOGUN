About Us

Dr. DK Olukoya. The General Overseer MFM Worldwide

Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries (MFM) is a global Christian organization founded on the principles of deep prayer, deliverance, and spiritual empowerment. With its headquarters in Lagos, Nigeria, MFM has a vision to propagate holiness and to bring people into a closer walk with God through fervent prayer and the power of the Word. Mountain of Fire and Miracles Campus Fellowship (MFMCF) at Olabisi Onabanjo University, Ibogun Campus, is an extension of this global vision tailored to empower students spiritually, socially, and academically. Our fellowship is a hub for young, vibrant believers who are passionate about living out their faith in a dynamic and supportive community...

Mountain of Fire and Miracles Campus Fellowship (MFMCF) OOU Ibogun is a faith-driven community dedicated to prayer, holiness, and spiritual empowerment. As the campus arm of Mountain of Fire and Miracles Ministries, we are committed to raising God-fearing, firebrand believers who excel both spiritually and academically. Our fellowship provides a platform for students to grow in faith, engage in intensive prayers, study the Word of God, and experience divine transformation. Through Bible teachings, worship, and evangelism, we equip students to stand strong in their Christian walk and fulfill their divine purpose. Join us and be part of a generation on fire for God!
